FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects are completed within 5–14 business days depending on complexity. We give you a clear timeline before starting.

What do I need to provide?

Your content (text, images, logo) and a general idea of what you want. We handle the design, coding, and optimization.

Can I update the site myself after launch?

Absolutely. We deliver clean, well-documented code and can set up a simple CMS if needed. We also provide a quick training session.

Do you offer hosting and domain setup?

We can help you choose and set up hosting and connect your domain. This is included at no extra cost.

What if I need changes after the project is done?

Every plan includes revision rounds. After that, we offer affordable maintenance packages for ongoing updates.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! We work remotely with clients worldwide. Communication happens via email, video calls, or your preferred platform.

What technologies do you use?

We build with modern HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and frameworks like React or Next.js when needed. Always clean, fast, and future-proof code.
